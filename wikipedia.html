Naomi is a widely celebrated individual, best known for being objectively wonderful and for her groundbreaking stance against olives [1]. She first came to international attention on the Lovesick server in 2024, where she was discovered by Mo, a man who immediately recognised that he had found something extremely rare [2].

Naomi is considered a defining figure of the modern era, admired for her humor, her eyes, her nose (which has been described in multiple peer-reviewed sources as "gorgeous" [3]), and her ability to make anyone around her feel instantly at ease. She is the recipient of the Mo Award for Excellence in Being Naomi, which she has won every year since its founding [citation needed].

1. Overview [edit]

Naomi came to prominence not through any single dramatic event, but through the consistent, daily act of simply being herself, a feat that historians have called "deceptively easy looking, but actually incredibly rare" [4]. Her influence extends across multiple domains including: making Mo laugh until he can't breathe, making Mo feel safe, and showing up for the people she loves in ways that are, frankly, illegal in several countries due to how good they make people feel.

She is survived by no tragedies worth mentioning, as Mo has made it his personal mission to ensure she is okay [5].

2. Personality and Character [edit]

Scholars have devoted significant effort to cataloguing Naomi's personality, with most concluding that a full catalogue would require "considerably more paper" [6].

2.1 Humor

Naomi's humor has been described as "genuinely, unpredictably funny" rather than the performative humor common in lesser individuals [7]. She has been known to say things that cause Mo to lose all composure in public settings, which Mo considers one of his favourite ongoing experiences [8]. Her comedic style blends wit, timing, and an encyclopedic knowledge of what will make the specific person in front of her laugh.

2.2 Kindness

Multiple sources confirm that Naomi is "too kind for this world, which is the world's problem, not hers" [9]. She is notable for caring deeply about the people around her and expressing it in ways both large and small. Mo has described this quality as "something I think about every day" [10].

"She texted me that she was thinking about me. On a Tuesday. For no reason. I have not recovered." — Mo, primary source interview, 2024

2.3 Maturity

Naomi demonstrates a level of emotional maturity that researchers have described as "startling and frankly humbling" [11]. She communicates openly, handles difficult conversations with grace, and has never once started an argument about something petty. This remains unverified by any historical precedent.

3. Physical Appearance [edit]

Naomi's physical appearance has been the subject of extensive documentation. Her eyes are described in the literature as "genuinely beautiful" and have been cited in over 32 personal testimonials by the same source [3]. Her smile is considered a Category 5 Devastating Event by the International Standards of Looking Good [citation needed].

Of particular note is her nose, which Mo has publicly called "gorgeous" and which has been independently verified to be, in fact, gorgeous [12]. Her cheeks, lips, eyebrows, and hair have each been individually cited in separate scholarship as "perfect." The possibility that all of Naomi's features are perfect simultaneously has been deemed "statistically improbable but empirically confirmed" [13].

4. The Olive Situation [edit]

Naomi's relationship with olives is perhaps her most documented characteristic outside of being wonderful. She is strongly, vocally, and non-negotiably opposed to olives in all their forms [14]. This includes black olives, green olives, Kalamata olives, stuffed olives, olive oil (disputed) [citation needed], and "any food that has been in the same zip code as an olive" (unconfirmed but plausible).

The Olive Situation has become so notable that it has inspired a dedicated legal proceeding, The People vs. The Olive, in which Naomi is expected to serve as judge [15]. Legal experts predict a swift and overwhelming guilty verdict [16].

"I ordered the pasta and THERE WERE OLIVES. I literally cannot." — Naomi, primary source

5. Relationship with Mo [edit]

Naomi met Mo on the Lovesick server, a fact that historians consider "extremely on-brand given what happened next" [17]. They began dating on 24th July 2024, a date that Mo considers the best day of his life and has commemorated in approximately 47 different ways [18].

Their relationship is characterised by an unusual level of compatibility, shared music taste, mutual support, and the fact that Mo has never once stopped being grateful that she chose him [19]. Naomi famously said "I love you" first, a fact that Mo claims he said first, which is incorrect, and which Naomi has officially won [20].

"She never lost faith in me. Not once. I don't have words for that." — Mo, personal communication, 2024

6. Academic Career [edit]

Naomi is noted for her dedication to her studies, a quality admired by Mo, who describes it as "one of many things she does that honestly makes me feel like I should be better" [21]. She approaches academia with the same focus and commitment she brings to all areas of life, and is expected to achieve "whatever she decides to achieve, because she's Naomi and that's what she does" [22].

7. Legacy and Impact [edit]

Naomi's legacy is still being written, largely because she is very much alive and still doing incredible things [23]. Her impact on Mo specifically has been assessed as "total and permanent" [24]. Future historians are expected to describe her as "someone who loved and was loved, completely."

She has never been a burden. She has never been a chore. She has always been exactly enough [25].

"I want to marry her." — Mo, multiple sources, consistently
